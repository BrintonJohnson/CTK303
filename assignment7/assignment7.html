Assignment 7: Birthday Party (Accessibility Fix)

"Too Close" by Next

About the Song

I was born on May 15, 1998. At this time, the song called "Too Close" by the R&B group, Next, was number one on Billboard's Hot 100. The song was released in September 1997 and went platinum, making it the group's most well-known song. The song uses a sample from Kurtis Blow's song, "Christmas Rappin'." I think "Too Close" is basically a romantic boy band song, which is typical 90's pop music.

Other Renditions

One interesting thing about this song is that the tune kind of resurfaced in 2015 through a parody called, "Why You Always Lying." This was a song created by Nicholas Fraser for a Vine video. The parody got very popular in 2015 on the internet, so that's what I know the "Too Close" tune from. I didn't know the parody sampled another song until I heard "Too Close" for this assignment.
